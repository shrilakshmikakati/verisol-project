RENTAL AGREEMENT
This Rental Agreement is made and executed on this 1st day of February, 2026, at Bengaluru, Karnataka between Mr. Ramesh, S/o Mr. Gajendra, residing at No. 45, 2nd Main Road, Rajaji Nagar, Bengaluru – 560010, hereinafter referred to as the "Landowner / Lessor", which expression shall mean and include his heirs, legal representatives, administrators, and assigns; and Mr. Suresh, S/o Mr. Aparshakti, residing at No. 112, 1st Cross, Chikkaballapur, Karnataka – 562101, hereinafter referred to as the "Tenant / Lessee", which expression shall mean and include his heirs, legal representatives, administrators, and assigns.
The Landowner and the Tenant are hereinafter collectively referred to as the "Parties".
1. PROPERTY DETAILS
The Landowner hereby agrees to rent out to the Tenant a 1 BHK residential house, situated at BTM Layout, 2nd Stage, Bengaluru, Karnataka – 560076, more fully described as follows:
    • Type: 1 Bedroom, Hall, Kitchen (1 BHK)
    • Floor: First Floor
    • Approx. Area: 550 sq. ft.
    • Electricity Meter No.: BLR-BTM-458921
    • Water Supply: BWSSB & Borewell
The premises shall be used only for residential purposes.
2. TERM OF TENANCY
The tenancy shall be for a fixed period of six (6) months, commencing from 01/02/2026 and ending on 31/07/2026, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement.
3. RENT
The Tenant agrees to pay a monthly rent of Rs. 18,000/- (Rupees Eighteen Thousand only), payable on or before the 5th day of every month, by bank transfer / UPI to the Landowner’s designated account.
4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant has paid a refundable security deposit of Rs. 60,000/- (Rupees Sixty Thousand only) to the Landowner, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged.
The security deposit shall be refunded at the time of vacating the premises after adjusting any dues towards rent, damages beyond normal wear and tear, electricity, water, or other charges.
5. MAINTENANCE & UTILITIES
    1. Monthly maintenance charges of Rs. 1,500/- shall be borne by the Tenant.
    2. Electricity charges shall be paid by the Tenant as per meter readings and applicable BESCOM rates.
    3. Water charges shall be paid by the Tenant as applicable.
    4. The Tenant shall maintain the premises in good condition and shall be responsible for minor day-to-day repairs.
6. USE OF PREMISES
The Tenant shall:
    • Use the premises only for residential purposes.
    • Not sublet, assign, or transfer the premises without written consent of the Landowner.
    • Not make any structural alterations without prior written permission.
    • Not engage in any illegal or unlawful activities on the premises.
7. LANDOWNER’S OBLIGATIONS
The Landowner shall:
    • Ensure peaceful possession and enjoyment of the premises during the tenancy period.
    • Carry out major structural repairs, if required.
    • Pay property taxes and other statutory dues related to ownership.
8. TERMINATION
Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving one (1) month prior written notice to the other Party.
Upon termination or expiry of the tenancy, the Tenant shall vacate the premises and hand over peaceful possession to the Landowner.
9. LOCK-IN PERIOD
This Agreement is for a fixed tenure of six (6) months. Early termination, if any, shall be subject to mutual discussion and settlement.
10. ENTRY & INSPECTION
The Landowner or his authorized representative may enter the premises with prior notice to inspect or carry out necessary repairs.
11. INDEMNITY
The Tenant agrees to indemnify the Landowner against any loss or damage caused to the premises due to negligence or misuse.
12. GOVERNING LAW & JURISDICTION
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Courts at Bengaluru shall have exclusive jurisdiction.
13. REGISTRATION
This Agreement may be notarized / registered as per applicable laws, and the cost shall be borne equally by both Parties unless otherwise agreed.
14. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This document constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior understandings, whether written or oral.
Any modification to this Agreement shall be made only in writing and signed by both Parties.
